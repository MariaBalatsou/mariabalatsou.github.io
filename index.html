Coming of Age

Read on Goodreads: Coming of Age

About the Book

It's her 39th birthday. She has it all. The man, the job, the flat. She sees the cracks on her skin. She refuses to see all her other cracks. Her inner voice, louder than ever and bewildered, makes fun of her, trying to speak reason over the impulsive life she is leading.

How will she survive the end of youth? When will she find the courage to face her boss? What could be waiting for her on the other side of a midlife crisis?

You are invited to two of her birthday parties, a decade apart, and the coming of age journey in between.
